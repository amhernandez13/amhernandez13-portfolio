CreSentia

CreSentia is a Colombian company focused on psychological well-being, offering personalized therapeutic programs that promote personal balance.

As part of my studies, I had the opportunity to contribute to the initial development of their upcoming web app.

In this project, I developed a registration form for one of CreSentia's user types, designed as a step-by-step process using a stepper format.

This customized form guides the user through each registration stage, completing the process by securely and efficiently sending all information to the backend.

URL: https://cresentia-register-form.vercel.app/
