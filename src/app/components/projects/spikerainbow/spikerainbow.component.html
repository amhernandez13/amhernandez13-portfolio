Spike Rainbow

Spike Rainbow is an artisanal bakery located in Cali, Colombia, that needed to expand its online presence through an ecommerce platform.

As part of my web development studies, I adapted a previous functional ecommerce project, redesigning the interface and tailoring the content to reflect Spike Rainbow's brand and products.

The result is an online store that provides an engaging and personalized experience for the bakery's customers.

(This site is under construction! The current design still reflects the base project. I'm actively working on updating it to the bakery theme.)
