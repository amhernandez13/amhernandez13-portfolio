ContaCol

Developed as part of the Smart Projects program by Foo Talent Group, ContaCol is a web app designed to simplify electronic invoice management in Colombia through automation.

Created for "Inversiones Agroambientales H&R", this application enables efficient management of the collection, storage, and payment validation of electronic invoices, ensuring accurate data extraction and minimizing human error.
